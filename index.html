CHICAGO CRIME SURPRISE MAPS

W209 Final Project

As a part of the Data Visualization & Communication course in UC Berkeley's Master of Information & Data Science (MIDS) program, we've developed a data visualization that can be used to geospatially display Surprise Ratios in regards to Chicago crime. Surprise ratios are a measurement of how the data at hand deviates from prior expectations and can be used to counteract biases in the data. For more information on surprise ratios, select the button in the top navigation bar.
